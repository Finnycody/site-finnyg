Tiny Home Design Challenge

Click here to see my house

I made this mini house using tinkercad in my Intro to engineering class. It is built to follow life-like dimensions and be able to fit on the road if it was turned into a trailer. This project allowed me to learn how to work under size restrictions and other requirements, like the necessity for a kitchen, bathroom, work space, and a bed, of course, all in a limited amount of space.
